Stack Concept (LIFO)

A Stack works on the principle of **Last In, First Out** (LIFO). The last element pushed is the first element popped.

  • ⚡ Push: Adds a new item to the top.
  • ⚡ Pop: Removes the top item.
  • ⚡ Peek: Views the top item without removing it.
